🦖 Welcome to Trex Studio Producer

Your complete music studio in a browser. Here's the 60-second tour:

1. Make a beat — Channel Rack (F6)

  • Each row is an instrument. Each square is a 16th note — click squares to turn them on.
  • Press Space to hear it loop. Classic starter: kick on steps 1, 5, 9, 13; clap on 5 and 13; hats on every odd step.
  • Knobs: left = volume, right = pan. Drag up/down. The green light mutes a channel.

2. Write a melody — Piano Roll (F7)

  • Pick a melodic channel (piano, bass, lead…), then click the grid to place notes. Right-click deletes; drag a note's right edge to stretch it.
  • Play the highlighted rows with your computer keyboard: A–K are white keys, W E T Y U are black keys, Z/X change octave.

3. Build the song — Playlist (F5)

  • Switch the transport to SONG mode. Paint your patterns onto the timeline tracks to arrange intro → verse → drop.
  • Click the ruler to jump anywhere (scrubbing); drag for fine control.

4. Mix it — Mixer (F9)

  • Every channel routes to a mixer insert. Drag faders, pan, mute/solo, and stack up to 8 effects per insert: EQ, delay, reverb, distortion, filter, compressor, chorus.

5. Record — Record (F8)

  • Pick your mic or audio interface, hit the big red button, perform, stop, then send the take to the Playlist.
